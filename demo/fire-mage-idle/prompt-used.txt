Prompt sent to GenerateImage:

A 2x2 sprite sheet (4 equal cells in a 2x2 grid) of a 16-bit pixel-art fire mage idle
animation, 3/4 view from slightly above. Subject identity: a young fire mage wearing a
deep crimson hooded robe with golden trim, holding a wooden staff topped with a small
floating ember; warm orange and red palette, dark crisp pixel outlines, saturated colors,
NOT cute, battle-ready stance.

Animation across 4 frames (read row-major):
- top-left: neutral idle, slight forward lean, ember glow at minimum
- top-right: subtle inhale, robe edge lifts a few pixels, ember slightly brighter
- bottom-left: small exhale, robe falls back, ember at peak with one tiny spark
- bottom-right: settled neutral pose returning to start, ember dimming back

ABSOLUTE RULES:
1. EXACTLY 4 equal quadrants in a 2x2 grid. NO borders, NO grid lines, NO frames between cells.
2. NO text, NO labels, NO numbers, NO arrows, NO UI, NO speech bubbles, NO watermark.
3. The character has the IDENTICAL bounding box and IDENTICAL pixel scale in every cell.
4. Character is centered in its cell, fills ~60% of the cell, equal magenta margin on all four sides.
5. Background is 100% solid flat #FF00FF magenta in every cell, no gradients, no shadow, no floor plane.
6. Entire character (including staff, ember, sparks, robe edges) must fit fully inside its own cell.
7. Same character identity, same outfit, same proportions in every frame; only the breathing/ember-pulse changes.

Square 1024x1024 image.

Postprocess command:

python3 ~/.cursor/skills/generate2dsprite/scripts/generate2dsprite.py process \
  --input raw-sheet.png \
  --target asset --mode idle \
  --rows 2 --cols 2 \
  --output-dir . \
